Licensing

Terms of use for visual materials and technological transparency.

All visual materials created by SENNS.STUDIO are produced using licensed generative technologies and proprietary production processes.

Usage rights

Upon full payment, the client receives the right to commercially use delivered visual materials without territorial or time restrictions, or as specified in the agreement, particularly in channels such as:

  • websites
  • social media
  • paid advertising
  • print
  • sales materials
  • marketplace and e-commerce

Ownership scope

Delivered images become part of the client visual communication. Proprietary production processes, tool configurations, workflows and technological solutions remain the property of the studio operator.

Resale and distribution

Materials may not be resold as a standalone visual product, stock or asset intended for further distribution without the written consent of the studio.

Nature of materials

All persons, faces and figures depicted in the images are synthetic. They do not represent real individuals, and any resemblance is coincidental.

Client data and materials

Files provided during cooperation are used exclusively for project execution. They are not used for model training or shared with third parties. After cooperation ends, materials may be deleted or archived upon client request.

Technological transparency

The studio operates based on licensed tools and current data protection and technological transparency standards. For public campaigns, it is recommended to label materials generated using AI technology in accordance with applicable market guidelines.
