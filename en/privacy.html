Privacy policy

Confidentiality and security of information shared during cooperation.

We take care of the confidentiality and security of information shared during cooperation. Personal data is processed only to the extent necessary for business contact, execution of visual projects and maintenance of communication related to studio services.

Data controller

The data controller is Optimattic, an entity operating a visual studio under the brand SENNS.STUDIO, registered in Poland.

Contact regarding data processing:
hello@senns.studio
NIP: 9562130534

Scope of processed data

Only data voluntarily provided by the user or client is processed, in particular:

  • email address
  • first and last name
  • company name
  • project information provided via message or during online meetings

The studio does not process sensitive data or information beyond the scope of cooperation.

Purpose of processing

Data is used exclusively for:

  • responding to inquiries
  • execution of visual projects
  • communication regarding cooperation
  • archiving project arrangements with client consent

Data retention period

Materials and project data are deleted by default after cooperation ends. Upon explicit client request, they may be securely archived for up to 12 months to facilitate future projects.

External tools

Calendly is used for scheduling meetings. Use of this service is subject to the privacy policy of its provider.

Cookies and technical logs

The website may use basic cookies and technical server logs for security, stability and traffic analysis purposes. This data is not combined with personally identifiable information.

Data security

Technical and organizational measures are in place to protect data against loss, unauthorized access or disclosure. Access to information is limited to the person responsible for project execution.

User rights

Every person has the right to:

  • access their data
  • rectification of data
  • erasure of data
  • restriction of processing
  • object to processing
  • data portability

Requests regarding the above can be submitted via email.

Cooperation confidentiality

Publication of work in the portfolio is done exclusively with the explicit consent of the client. Upon request, full anonymization of materials, no brand markings and non-disclosure of production source is possible. The studio operates as a production backend and respects cooperation confidentiality principles.
