Technical standards

Quality parameters and production processes of visual materials.

SENNS.STUDIO executes visual projects based on controlled production processes, generative tools and proprietary workflows ensuring aesthetic consistency and high technical quality of materials.

Material realism and color accuracy

The image generation process is aimed at faithful reproduction of fabrics, surfaces and light. Special emphasis is placed on:

  • material texture
  • natural light behavior
  • color accuracy matching client references
  • silhouette proportions and product geometry

For optimal results, we recommend providing reference photos, color samples or product materials.

Input requirements

Materials provided for production should be of the highest possible quality. We recommend:

  • product photos on a uniform background
  • even lighting
  • no strong shadows or reflections
  • minimum resolution 1024 px on the shorter side

Output format and resolution

Standard delivery format includes:

  • images in JPEG, PNG, TIFF format
  • base resolution 2048 px with upscaling to 4K and higher
  • aspect ratios tailored to publication channels

Layered files are not a standard delivery element.

Data security and confidentiality

Project materials are used exclusively within the given project. Data is not used for model training or shared with third parties. File access is limited to the person responsible for project execution.

Technological compliance

The production process is based on licensed tools and solutions compliant with applicable data protection and technological transparency standards. The studio monitors regulatory changes regarding generative technologies and adapts processes to current legal requirements.

Diversity and visual neutrality

Projects can be executed with consideration for diversity of body types, visual characteristics and styles aligned with the client brand identity. Upon request, models can be tailored to specific visual guidelines.

Production efficiency and flexibility

The generative process enables rapid creation of visual variants without the need to reproduce materials. This allows flexible testing of creative directions and optimization of production costs.

Support and communication

Every project is executed in direct communication with the client. Response time for operational inquiries typically does not exceed one business day.
